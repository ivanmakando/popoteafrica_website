What does word POPOTE means?

Popote is a swahili word that means 'Everywhere'. Our target as Popote Africa Adventure Ltd is to become the best tour agency in Africa. We want to serve our clients to the bestselling of Africa. As a company we intends to group and become the best tour agency in Africa and that is why we carry the name of Popote Africa Adventures Ltd.

Our Services

Our guiding principle is to work with each client, family and group in an individual way. We encourage you to work with us, discussing your interests, style of travel, budget, time frame and any other factors that would influence your trip – to come up with an itinerary best suited to you, your family or group. We true expert when it comes for Tanzania safari and Kilimanjaro climb, we offer the best value for your money.

We provide 7-seater custom safari Land cruisers with pop-up roof ideal for game viewing and sightseeing. The vehicles are well maintained to provide maximum level of comfort and safety and are fitted with HF long range radios for easy communication. The vehicles are fully equipped with cool-boxes, charging systems, Binocular and first aid kits, as well as guide books on wildlife, birds and plants of East Africa.

To make it is easy and convenient for the traveler, all our safaris and climbs depart daily with no restriction in booking. We do this to allow our itineraries/safaris/climbs fit into your schedule, and prices are based on number of people in a travel group, so more participants more affordable. Please provide us with this information during your first enquiry to help us send you a most appropriate quotation.

Our Guides

We know this for a fact! The success of your safari/climb will largely depend on your driver/guide and the team in general. That is why we have a great team of carefully selected driver guides and Kilimanjaro guides who are trained and experienced and with a vast knowledge of the wildlife and animal behavior as well as the local fauna and flora.

Our Kilimanjaro guides are all KINAPA trained and licensed, and specifically chosen for our upbeat and adaptable personalities. More importantly, we know the importance of acclimatization and will make sure you trek "pole pole - slow slow", giving you the best chance of summiting the mountain.
